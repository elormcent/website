ABOUT US

FAST LINK

Fast Link was incorporated in Ghana in 18th July, 2019 by the investment of HTU Corporation, Ghana and Mugi Auto S.A., Benin. The company started operations in 18th July, 2019. In , HTU Corporation, Ghana became the sole shareholder of the company.

Octagon is the sole authorized distributor for HTU Corporation of HO. The principal activity of Octagon is the sale of new site, and the provision of After-Sales service support. As the sole authorized distributor for Fast Link Corporation, Octagon staff are equipped with the requisite skill that enhances quality of service to meet Fast Link Global Standards. Today, the company became the sole authorized distributor of Auto networks, providing after-sales support for customers. Octagon has branches in - Accra, Tema, Kumasi and Tarkwa. In addition, Octagon has appointed five (5) workshops thus; Ansuare Company Limited in Tamale, Kwansa Auto Ltd in Takoradi, Gerco Auto Ventures in Ho, Cape Coast Technical Institute in Cape Coast and Eusbett Service Centre in Sunyani as authorized Service Shops (TGASS).
